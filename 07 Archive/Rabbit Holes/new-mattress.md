## Research

**Bottom Line:** Mattress buying is deeply personal (sleep position, body weight, budget all matter), but the current consensus leans toward **hybrid mattresses** (foam + coils) for most people — they balance pressure relief, support, and cooling better than pure memory foam. Top-rated picks in 2026 are the **Helix Midnight Luxe** (best overall) and **Bear Elite Hybrid** (most-mentioned on Reddit). Avoid buying without at least a 100-night trial period — all the major online brands offer this. Budget sweet spot is $1,000–$1,800; above that you're paying for brand name. GoodBed.com is the most trustworthy research tool — uses objective machine testing, not just subjective reviews.

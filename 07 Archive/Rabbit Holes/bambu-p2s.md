## Research

**Bottom Line:** The P2S launched October 2025 at $549 (standalone) / $799 (Combo with AMS 2 Pro). It's a real upgrade over the P1S — better extruder, quick-swap nozzle, bigger touchscreen, improved camera — but the community consensus for P1S owners is "not worth it unless something is bugging you." The one compelling case is the $799 Combo if your original AMS is giving trouble; the AMS 2 Pro actively dries filament faster. Otherwise, keep the P1S.

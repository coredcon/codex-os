## Research

**Bottom Line:** Star Wars is back in theaters with its clearest near-term roadmap in years. Two films are locked — *The Mandalorian & Grogu* (May 2026) and *Starfighter* (May 2027) — with four more in various stages of development. The franchise is deliberately leaving the sequel trilogy behind and opening multiple new eras simultaneously. Disney is internally more confident in Starfighter than Mando & Grogu.

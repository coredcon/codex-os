# *|CSH| - Unable to connect to your ConnectSmartHost Server. Check your network connectivity and DineTime configuration.

**Issue:**  User launches DineTime Host for IOS, receives error: "Unable to connect to your DineTime Host Server. Check your network connectivity and DineTime configuration."


**Resolution****: **


- If the customer is using DineTime Server, see article in **DineTime Server - Troubleshooting**:
[Unable to connect to your DineTime Host Server. Check your network connectivity and DineTime configuration]


- If the customer is a Standalone/Cloud user, follow the steps below to turn off Server mode:

- Open Settings app on iPad.

- Scroll down to the settings for DineTime Host on the left.

- Toggle the "Use Local DineTime Server" switch off (not green).

- Relaunch DineTime Host App

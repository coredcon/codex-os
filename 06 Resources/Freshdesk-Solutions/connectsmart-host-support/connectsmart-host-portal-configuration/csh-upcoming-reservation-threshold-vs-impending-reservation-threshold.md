# *|CSH| - Upcoming Reservation Threshold vs Impending Reservation Threshold

Both settings can be found on the DineTime Portal after selecting a site under CUSTOMIZE >> RESERVATIONS.


These settings determine when and how a Reservation will be presented on the _Waiting_ tab of the Hostess application.


**Upcoming Reservation Threshold**:


This setting controls the amount of time that the DineTime Host application will look ahead to determine when a Reservation will be listed under the _Waiting_ tab in a section defined "_Upcoming Reservations_".


Example:


Reservation, Party of 2, 12:00 PM EST.


Current site time is 11:03 AM EST.


DineTime Portal setting is "3 Hours".


All Reservation within the next 3 hours of current time (11:03 AM) will be now shown at bottom of Waiting tab:


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


**Impending Reservation Threshold:**


This setting controls the amount of time that the DineTime Host application will look ahead to determine when a Reservation will be moved from the _Waiting_ tab section "_Upcoming Reservations_" into the current working wait list.


Example (same reservation used in example above):


Reservation, Party of 2, 12:00 PM EST.


Current site time is 11:30 AM EST.


DineTime Portal setting is "30 Minutes".

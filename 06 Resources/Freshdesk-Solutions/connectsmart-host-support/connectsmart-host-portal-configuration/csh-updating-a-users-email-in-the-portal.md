# *|CSH| - Updating a User's Email in the Portal

## Goal/Desired Behavior:


Updated email address for a user account in the Company Portal.


## Steps:


- Navigate to the Company Portal 

- From the Company tab go to  Users 

- Then select the desire user > Click pencil icon 

- Enter the new email address and click Done.

- 


- A verification email will be sent to that address and the user will need to click the link within that email to verify the address.


**NOTE: Never make a change for a user/client, either walk them through how to make the change themselves or direct them to their System Admin. If there is no System Admin then reach out to help_support/help_afterhours Slack channel for assistance.**

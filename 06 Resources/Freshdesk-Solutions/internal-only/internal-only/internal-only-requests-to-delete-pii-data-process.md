# *|Internal Only| - Requests to Delete PII Data - Process

From time to time we will receive requests from a restaurant customer or an integrated partner on behalf of one of their guests requesting that any Personally Identifiable Information about them be removed.  Since restaurants' guest data is stored in DineTime, QSR has an obligation to accommodate.


These requests will be handled by the Technical Projects team which will follow the following process:


- Search the customer's Guestbook for any records that are exact matches or approximate matches to the information provide.

- Reply to the customer with the number of exact matches and approximate matches and ask for clarification on how they would like to proceed.  Redact any specific PII information like the guest's name downthread from your replies, unless you must specifically refer to it to ask a question.  Use vague language like "The last name provided" instead of replying with the last name.

- If the customer would like to proceed with deleting any portion of the matches, submit a Resource Request DE ticket and provide the specific records that require deletion.

- When Dev confirms the records are deleted, they will redact the guest's information from the DE ticket.

- Reply to the requestor and confirm the guest records have been deleted.

- Redact the guest information from the ticket completely and delete all email correspondence

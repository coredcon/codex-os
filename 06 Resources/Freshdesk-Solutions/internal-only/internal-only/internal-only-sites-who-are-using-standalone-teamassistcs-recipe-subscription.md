# *|Internal Only| - Sites who are using standalone TeamAssist/CS Recipe Subscription

It is possible that sites will choose to only us TeamAssist/ConnectSmart Recipe where the subscription will look as followed:


The SF Product code will be 001-7825 and will be listed as CS Recipes (formerly TeamAssist) Stand-Alone. With this subscription there is nothing that needs to be installed on the server side and everything is access purely through the portal.


The customer will need to configure all their recipes in the Recipe portal and ensure that their user accounts have access to the concept(s) they have created recipes for in the portal. 


Then when they are ready to use the recipe viewer they will navigate to [https://viewer.teamassist.qsr.cloud/] and login with their portal account that has access to the recipe concept they have created. Once they are logged in and assuming have their TA/Recipe Concept enabled for that account they should be able to see their recipes. 


(Note: if they have more than one concept they will first be given an option to select one of the concepts to view)

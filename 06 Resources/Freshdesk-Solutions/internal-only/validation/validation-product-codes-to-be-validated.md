# *|Validation| Product Codes to be Validated

The link below will take you to the ProductCodes.xlsx document containing all OLD and NEW product codes that must be validated in Salesforce.  The information is broken up into multiple tabs - please be sure match codes to ensure you are using the proper tab.


The ProductCodes.xlsx file lives [here].   


Now, more than ever, it will be important to keep the ProductCodes.xlsx open to confirm the correct components are enabled on every license request.  Always/Only open the spreadsheet from the SupportTeam SharePoint site to ensure you are looking at the most up-to-date information.  If you choose to save it locally the copy will not update if/when changes are made to the original on SharePoint.

**New Features/Enhancements:**


**Supports Kitchen SMS**


-  Kitchen software should be able to trigger SMS notifications to guests' mobile phone numbers provided through kitchen's POS API to notify guests when orders are "ready." The event that triggers when the order is considered "ready" should be configurable (may be order prepared, order assembled, order bumped....). Our APIs should include a way for a guest to opt out of receiving SMS notifications. 


**Supports dine-in future orders**


**- Dine-In Future Orders are orders a customer places in advance with the intention of eating in the restaurant. For example, a guest may call a restaurant to put their name on the wait list and decide to place their order as well, to save time once they’ve arrived. The restaurant may wish to hold this order until the guest has arrived or been seated, to ensure a smoother dining experience.  **


**Supports Metrics-based Capacity Management**


**- ** U**se "realtime" kitchen metrics to adjust capacity management thresholds dynamically for a limited number of capacity periods in the future. **

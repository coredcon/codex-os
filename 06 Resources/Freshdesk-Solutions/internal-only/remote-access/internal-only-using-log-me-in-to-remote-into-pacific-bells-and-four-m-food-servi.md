# *|Internal Only| - Using Log Me In to remote into Pacific Bells and Four M Food Service Sites

There are instances where remote access can be established at specific Buffalo Wild Wings (BWW) sites without customer action and the two specific Re-seller/Franchise organizations are **Pacific Bells** and **Four M Food Service**.


 


- When taking a BWW call it is a good procedure to look up the site in LMTS and verify the site’s franchise and if they are under either of the above franchises then you should be able to login to their server via [Log Me In] (see below screenshot):


 


 


 


 


- After visiting the Log Me In web page you will want to click the "Login" option that is in the top right hand of the screen:


 


 


 


 


 


- Login using the following credentials: _(If the password expires, please reset it and then inform the support team of the new password using the Support Distro email)_

- **Username:** support@qsrautomations.com

- **Password:**  Support@2022!! 


 


 


 


- After clicking "Login" you will be prompted to enter a security code that is automatically sent to the On Call phone via text, however in most instances you will need to click the "Get code via email" link that can be found underneath the Verify button:


 


 


 


- After clicking the Get code via email link you will then want to check the junk folder in the Support email account, retrieve the code, and then enter that code into the prompt and press Verify when finished. (see screenshot for example)


 


    


 


 


- Now after logging in you will find that each site has been organized into lists according to their Franchise and you can navigate between the franchises by clicking the drop down menu at the top and selecting the franchise you wish to look at:


 


 


 


- When trying to remote into either Four M or Pacific Bells servers you will be prompted to enter the following login credentials:

- **Four M Food Service:**

- Username: N/A

- Password: 12345678


- **Pacific Bells:**

- Username: BWW[Site Number] (ex. BWW1234)

- Password:  BWW[Site Number]


 


- There is always a chance that some of the older franchises that are listed in LMTS have been acquired by Pacific Bells or Four M. Because of this it is good practice to check and see if the site you are working with is setup in Log Me In if you are unable to connect to them via Zoho

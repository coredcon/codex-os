# * |CSK| - Communication issues with Aloha 15.1 

**Issue: **


The Aloha 15.1 release had a number of issues that resulted in the POS not communicating properly with ConnectSmart Kitchen. 


**Resolution: **


Aloha POS released version 15.1.27 of their POS software which resolved the communication issues with CSK. If any sites are reporting issues after upgrading their Aloha to 15.1, advise them they will need to be on at least 15.1.27.

# CSK - Bump "from" configuration

When a customer wants to have one Kitchen station bump from another.


**Step 1:
**


Once connected to the customer's server, open ConnectSmart Kitchen Builder Pro and choose the desired dataset to review.


**Step 2:**

Click on the "Activity Levels" tab and open the desired station to setup the "Bump From" configuration.


**Step 3:**

Right click or double click on the desired station and choose "Edit", select the Bumping tab and then choose the station to "Bump from", click OK.


**Step 4: **


After applying the change to the desired station, click on "Tools" then choose "copy to run time", finally after restarting the QSR Kitchen server service, the customer changes will take effect.

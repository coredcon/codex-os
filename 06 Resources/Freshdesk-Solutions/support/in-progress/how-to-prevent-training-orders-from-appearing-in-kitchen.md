# How to prevent Training Orders from appearing in Kitchen

Issue/Scenario: When training to submit orders on the POS the site doesn't want the orders to show up in the kitchen; or during Training Week the Insights Reports are skewed.


Requirements: POS sending order needs to be in Training Mode(Sends a header for the Order to indicate it is for Training).

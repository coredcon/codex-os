# Cyber123 - FAQs

Cyber123 is an End User that supports Giggling Squid locations based out of the UK. We tend to receive emails from this Support Team on a frequent basis regarding several issues. Here are a few that have frequently been encountered by our Support Team.


 


            **Why can’t store send Deliveroo Orders to QSR?:**


- Confirm that the only orders that cannot send to the kitchen are Deliveroo Orders.

- Advise site to contact Deliveroo service to validate that the platform has been integrated with the Site location ID from zonal/ Comtrex in order for it to send orders to QSR

- Ticket (s) reference: 359899


 


            **Who do I contact to replace a Display Monitor in the Kitchen?**


- This request should be sent to the Cyber123 Support team due to them being responsible for replacing the Display Monitors in the store.

- Confirm that the issue is with the monitor and not the Controller.

- Ticket (s) reference: 360070, 359375, 354171, 354983


            **Who is responsible for making routing changes to the Dataset for Giggling Squid locations?**


- Confirm with UK Implementations that the site’s Dataset is supported by Giggling Squid support or QSR.

- There is a very small list of site’s that our UK implementation team supports, but the majority of Giggling Squid locations have their Dataset supported by their support team.

- The point of contact that frequently appears within our Support for Dataset changes of Giggling Squid locations: saurabh@gigglingsquid.com

- Ticket(s) reference: 358382, 358419, 324284, 324300, 310021

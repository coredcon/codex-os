# Automated SMS notifications are unable to be sent to Guests

**Issue:** Site has reported that they are unable to send automated SMS notifications to Guests.


 


**Problem:** The site does not have the notifications enabled to send automated SMS notifications, or the site does not have Synchronized Setting Visit Events enabled within the Admin Portal.


 


**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Confirm that the site does support SMS within SalesForce, and has at least one of the following Automated SMS notifications enabled within the Host Portal > Sites > Customize > Notifications settings:


2. Confirm if the site does have "Visit Events" enabled within the Admin Portal > Sites > Site Settings > Synchronization settings:


** This feature allows Automatic SMS notification events to be sent to Guests. **

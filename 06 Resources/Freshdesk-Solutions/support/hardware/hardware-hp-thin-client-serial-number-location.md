# *|Hardware| - HP Thin Client Serial Number Location

The serial number for the HP Thin Clients can be found under the bottom panel cover:


Devices sold before sometime in Mid 2022 will have a QSR Serial Number beginning with 24-XXXXXX


Devices sold more recently will not have a QSR serial number and will only have the manufacturer serial number which is what we currently use for these devices.

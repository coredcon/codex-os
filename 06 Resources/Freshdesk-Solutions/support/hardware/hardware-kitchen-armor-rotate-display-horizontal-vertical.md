# *|Hardware| - Kitchen Armor - Rotate Display - Horizontal / Vertical

KA Support provided the following steps,

1. Navigate to the settings application2. Go to "Display"3. Click on "Screen Rotate" and change to the desired orientation4. Click on "When The Device Is Rotated" and set it to "Stay In Current Orientation"
These steps remain consistent with all of the KA POE devices.

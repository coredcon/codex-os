# *|Hardware| - Pairing a Bluetooth Keypad to an HP ThinClient

**Pairing a Bluetooth Keypad to an HP ThinClient**


 


This document will walk you through the process of getting your KP-9000 keypad to an HP Thin Client


**Pairing Process: **


 


- Open the start menu and search for Bluetooth

- Click on Bluetooth and other Device Settings
 
 


 


- Click on Ad Bluetooth or other devices at the top of the screen
 
 


 
 


- Click on Bluetooth
 
 


 


- Put the Keypad into pairing Mode by holding button 1 and button 20 until the light starts flashing Amber.

- Buttons 1 and 18 would be used to Unpair the keypad if it was previously paired to a different device.
 
 


 


- Look for the device to show up as “QSR Keypad” in the list of available devices on the controller.
 
 


 


- The keypad should now be paired and ready for use.

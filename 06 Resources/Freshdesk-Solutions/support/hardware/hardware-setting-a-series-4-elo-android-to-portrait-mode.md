# *|Hardware| - Setting a Series 4 ELO Android to Portrait mode

**This can now be managed through ControlPoint:**
****


Manual Steps just in case they are needed:


Press the Power and Home buttons at the same time to access android settings:


Click on the screen to get to the password screen:


Enter the password: 1qsra


Click on Settings:


Click on Display:


Click on Advanced:


Click on Screen Orientation:


Select the Desired Screen orientation (90 is portrait, 0 is Standard):


Once you have selected the desired Orientation back out until you get to the screen where you selected Settings and click on the menu on the top right:


Click the X in the top right:


The Kitchen screen should come back up after that.

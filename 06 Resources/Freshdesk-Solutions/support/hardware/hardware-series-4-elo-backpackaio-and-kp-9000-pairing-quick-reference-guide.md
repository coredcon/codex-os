# *|Hardware| - Series 4 ELO Backpack/AIO and KP-9000 Pairing Quick Reference Guide

**Series 4 ELO Backpack/AIO and KP-9000 Pairing Quick Reference Guide**


 


Follow these steps to pair a KP-9000 Keypad directly to an ELO Backpack or Elo All in One


**Pairing Process: **


- **Press the Power and Home Buttons together to get to the Android Settings**

- This may take several attempts as it can be tricky to get them both to press together.
 
 
 
 

- **Click on the screen to get to a login:**

- The password will be **1qsra**
 
 
 
 


- **Click on Settings
 
 **


**
 **


- **Click on Connected Devices**


** **


**
 **


- **Click on Pair New Device
 
 **


**
 **


- **Put the Keypad into pairing Mode by holding button 1 and button 20 until the light starts flashing Amber.**

- Buttons 1 and 18 would be used to Unpair the keypad if it was previously paired to a different device.


**
 **


- **Look for the device to show up as “QSR Keypad” in the list of available devices on the controller.**

- Click on it to start pairing to the keypad.
 
 


**
 **


- **You will get a pop-up asking if you want to allow access to contacts and call history, check the box for allow and click Pair
 
 **


**
 **


- **Wait for the pairing to complete and the verify that you see it listed under Currently connected:
 
 **


**
 **


- **Back out to the menu were you chose the Settings App and click on the menu to the top right
 
 **


**
 **


- **Click the X in the top right
 
 **


**
 **


- **The keypad should now be paired and ready for use.**

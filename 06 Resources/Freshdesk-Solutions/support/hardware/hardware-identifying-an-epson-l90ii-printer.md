# |Hardware| - Identifying an Epson L90II Printer

**Purpose:**
To verify whether a customer's Epson printer is an L90II model.


**Instructions:**


- 

**Locate the White Sticker**
Ask the customer to check the **white sticker on the side of the printer**. This label typically contains model and serial number information.


- 

**Check the Three-Digit Number**
At the top of the label, there will be a **three-digit number**.


- 

**Verify the Model**


- 

If the **three-digit number begins with "68"**, then the printer is an **Epson L90II**.


- 

Example: **681, 682, 683** all indicate an L90II.


**Note:**
If the label is missing or unclear, additional verification methods may be needed.

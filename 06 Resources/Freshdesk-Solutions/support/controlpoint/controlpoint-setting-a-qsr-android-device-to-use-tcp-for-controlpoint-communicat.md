# *|ControlPoint| - Setting a QSR Android device to use TCP for ControlPoint Communication

This article should be effective for any QSR Android devices after the phase two release.


From the DisplayClient screen choose the white DA button in the bottom right corner:


From the white DeviceAgent screen choose the purple **<...>** button in the bottom right corner of the screen:


**NOTE: **On newer versions, the buttons have been consolidated into a single gear icon:


In that version, you will need to then select the ControlPoint Connection Settings option:
 


Choose the option for **Use the following IP address (TCP-Only): **and enter the IP address for the ControlPoint server, the Base Port will be 32768:


Click Save to confirm the options.

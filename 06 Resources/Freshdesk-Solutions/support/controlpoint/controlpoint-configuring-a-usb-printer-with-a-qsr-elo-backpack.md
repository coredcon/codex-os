# *|ControlPoint| - Configuring a USB printer with a QSR ELO Backpack

**Configuring a USB printer with a QSR ELO Backpack.**


 


This process will start with the device assigned and online in ControlPoint and the printer (Epson and Bixilon printers are currently supported) connected to the controller with a direct USB connection (dongles are NOT supported) but not powered on. You will also need a printer template for the printer, this is done under the Printers option to the top right in ControlPoint.


- Edit the Device in ControlPoint and go to the printing tab.


Choose your printer and change the Manufacturer to Auto Detect. (In some cases, you may need to specify the manufacturer, Epson printers will almost always need to be selected manually)


 
Power on the printer.

The printer should now be assigned to the device in ControlPoint and should print when triggered from ConnectSmart Kitchen.

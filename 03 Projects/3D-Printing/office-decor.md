# Office Decor — Print Ideas

> **Target aesthetic: Wizard's Study** — arcane instruments, aged scholar's artifacts, mystical but grounded. Not Halloween. Not cheesy gothic.
> Sources: MakerWorld and Printables.
> Note: model images couldn't be embedded (sites block scraping) — click links to preview.

## Office Context

- **Govee blue LED lighting** throughout — two zones: theater side and desk/shelf side
- **Theater side:** Large projector screen, couch, two small corner shelves flanking the screen — needs strong silhouette pieces, reads from a distance. Tealight lanterns work great here.
- **Desk/shelf side:** Dragon head wall mount, book shelves, minis, bell jar lamp, Vox Arcanum hollow book (custom), glass orbs
- **Filament available:** Clear/transparent, plus whatever's in the AMS
- **Tealights:** Rechargeable tealights used throughout — wall sconces (left wall), candle holders
- **Avoid:** Cheesy/Halloween adjacent — skull sconces, cauldrons, cartoonish pieces, generic "goth"

## Do Not Revisit (Cheesy / Wrong Vibe)

- Skull Sconce LED Tea Light Holder
- Cauldron Tea Light Holder
- Gengar Candle Lamp
- Halloween Tealight Lantern
- Mini Pumpkin Lantern
- Gothic Lithophane Light (too craft-fair)
- Medieval Wall-Mounted Tealight Holder (too generic)
- LED Candle Lantern Framing (too busy)
